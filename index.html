Project name:Vehicle Insurance Claim Fraud Detection

Descrpition

Vehicle insurance fraud significantly threatens the financial stability of insurance companies and the fairness of premiums for honest customers. This project aims to tackle this issue by developing a machine learning model to detect fraudulent vehicle insurance claims. Leveraging algorithms such as Decision Trees and K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), the model analyzes accident data and policy history to distinguish between genuine and fake claims. By training the model on a comprehensive dataset of both real and fraudulent claims, it learns to recognize subtle signs of fraud. This predictive system operates in real-time, flagging suspicious claims for human review, helping insurance companies save money, streamline claim processing, and ensure fair premiums for all policyholders. This proactive approach enhances the accuracy of fraud detection, ultimately safeguarding financial sustainability and customer trust in the vehicle insurance industry.
